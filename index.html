About Orange Jordan

Orange Jordan is one of Orange Group’s subsidiaries in 26 countries, one of the world’s top ten operators, with 137,000 employees serving a total customer base of 298 million customers worldwide (as on 31 December 2023) and sales of 44.1 billion euros at the end of 2023. Orange offers a wide range of information technology and telecommunication services not only to the individuals, but also to businesses under the brand Orange Business. As the digital partner of the Kingdom, Orange continues to drive digital transformation by introducing the most advanced technologies, robust infrastructure and innovative solutions, available through its 61 shops and 38 smart selling points across the kingdom, as well as its various digital channels. Orange Jordan remains committed to empowering communities through its comprehensive corporate social responsibility strategy based on four pillars: digital inclusion, digital education, entrepreneurship and climate & environment, with a focus on youth, women, and persons with disabilities. The main Orange digital programs comprise the Community Digital Centers, BIG accelerator, Coding Academy, Fabrication Labs, and the inclusive umbrella “Differently Abled, Definitely Enabled” for persons with disabilities. Seeking to expand free digital programs across the Kingdom, currently available in more than 50 locations and through the e-platform, Orange Jordan working to reinforce digital inclusion, foster innovation, and enable youth.
